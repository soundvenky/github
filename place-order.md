# Place Your First Order

This guide walks you through ordering food on QuickBite 
for the first time.

---

## Prerequisites

Make sure you have completed the following:

- Created your QuickBite account
- Added a delivery address
- Added a payment method

---

## Search for Restaurants
Perform the following steps to search for restaurant or a dish:

1. Open the QuickBite app on your phone.
2. Click the **Search** field at the top of the **Home** screen.
3. Enter the name of a restaurant or a dish.
4. Click the preferred restaurant from the results to open its menu.

> **Tip:** You can also browse by category - click icons like
> **Biryani**, **Pizza**, or **Desserts** on the **Home** screen.

---

## Add Items to Cart
Perform the following steps to add items to your cart:

1. Browse the menu and add the item you prefer.
3. Click **Add to Cart**.
4. Repeat for any additional items.
5. Click the **Cart** icon at the bottom to review your order.

> **Note:** Items in your cart are saved for 30 minutes.
> After that, your cart will be cleared automatically.

---

## Review the Order

On the cart screen, check the following before proceeding:

| What to check | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| Item names and quantities | Listed in the cart |
| Delivery address | Below the item list |
| Estimated delivery time | Shown at the top |
| Total amount | At the bottom |

To remove an item, tap the `−` button next to it.

---

## Apply a Coupon
Perform the following steps to apply discount to the cart items:

1. Click **Apply Coupon** in the **Cart** screen.
2. Enter your coupon code in the **Apply Coupon** field.
3. Click **Apply**.
4. The discount will reflect in the total bill at the bottom.

> **Note:** Only one coupon can be applied per order.

---

## Choose a Payment Method
Perform the following steps to pay and place an order:

1. Select **Proceed to Payment**.
2. Select your preferred payment method:
   - UPI
   - Credit or debit card
   - Cash on delivery
3. Pay with the preferred payment method.
4. You will see an **Order Confirmed** screen with your order ID.

---

## What happens next

Once your order is placed:

- You will receive a confirmation notification.
- You can track your delivery in real time from the **Home** screen.
- The restaurant will begin preparing your order immediately.

---

## Troubleshooting

**Payment failed but amount was deducted?**
Wait 10 minutes. The amount will be refunded automatically.
If not, contact support from **Profile --> Help --> Payment Issue.**

**Wrong item added to cart?**
Tap the cart icon → tap `−` to remove the item before placing 
the order. Orders cannot be edited after confirmation.
