# Getting Started with QuickBite

QuickBite helps you order food from restaurants near you 
and get it delivered to your door in under 30 minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

- An Android or iOS smartphone
- A working phone number
- An internet connection

---

## Workflow

- Download Quickbite
- Create an Account
- Next Steps

### Download Quickbite
Perform the following steps to download quickbite:

1. Open the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play Store** (Android).
2. Search for `QuickBite` in the **Search** field.
3. Click **Install**.

> **Note:** QuickBite is free to download.

---

### Create an Account
Perform the following steps to create an account:
1. Open the QuickBite app.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Enter your mobile number and select **Send OTP**.
4. Enter the 6-digit OTP you receive.
5. Fill in your name and email address.
6. Click **Create Account**.  

> **Tip:** Use the same number you use for UPI payments 
> to make checkout faster later.

---

### Next steps

Once your account is set up, you are ready to [Place your first order](./place-order.md).
